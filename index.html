About The Server

Welcome to
The Borough

The Borough is a serious, immersive FiveM roleplay server built by roleplayers, for roleplayers. Set in a living, breathing city. Every shift, every case, every deal matters.

🚓

Metropolitan Police

Full Sonoran CAD integration, realistic traffic stops, detailed police procedures, multiple divisions including RPU, CID, ERPT and AFO.

🏥

London Ambulance Server

A fully developed medical experience with advanced injury systems, hospital roleplay, and high quality training ensuring high quality standards around the clock.

⚖️

Court Roleplay

Active court system with judges, lawyers, and prosecutors. Every charge can be contested. Justice is earned, not given.

💼

Civilian Life

Over 50 civilian jobs from mechanic to real estate broker, an immersive roleplay experience for all to enjoy with realistic housing simulation.

🌆

Custom Map & Assets

Unique MLOs, custom interiors, and exclusive vehicle liveries make The Borough unlike any other server you've played on. We strive to ensure your experience is as smooth as possible with the most realistic touch.

🛡️

Moderation Team

A strong moderation team ensuring that the rules of The Borough are kept the highest standard, and your questions are awnsered from experience.

🎯

High Roleplay Standards

At The Borough, roleplay standards should be kept to the highest standard. We ensure immersive roleplay is kept throughout in events and interactions.

🔧

Dedicated Development

Regular updates in our development blog, custom scripts built in house, active developer team, and a direct feedback pipeline from community to development.
