Server Rules

All roleplay must be realistic, immersive, and value long term storytelling.
Powergaming, metagaming, exploiting, or finding rule loopholes is not tolerated.
Respect all players and staff members of the community. The community is the forefront of our values, we don't want toxcicity breaking it.
Do not engage in any sexual, homophobic and racist roleplay. This includes the roleplay of pregnancies & child birth.
Moderational decisions are final to ensure server stability and fairness.
